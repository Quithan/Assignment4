Espresso

A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans. It forms the base for many other coffee drinks.

$8.99

Latte

An espresso-based drink with steamed milk and a small amount of milk foam on top. Lattes can be flavored with syrups such as vanilla or caramel.

$8.99

Cappuccino

Similar to a latte but with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It is often sprinkled with cocoa powder, cinnamon, or nutmeg on top.

$8.99

Americano

Made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to drip coffee but a different flavor profile.

$8.99

Macchiato

An espresso with just a dash of steamed milk or foam, creating a strong coffee flavor with a hint of creaminess.

$8.99

Flat White

Originating from Australia/New Zealand, a flat white consists of a shot of espresso with velvety microfoam milk, creating a strong coffee flavor with a smooth, creamy texture.

$8.99
